Rammstein

More recently (about two years ago), a German friend of mine introduced me to Rammstein, which I'm sure you've already heard of. Turns out it's part of a genre called 'Neue Deutsche Härte' (NDH). They're pretty well-known for their song 'Du Hast' from 1997, but I'm more of a fan of their more recent work.

Like their more recent songs:

  • Deutschland
  • Sonne
  • Mein Herz Brennt
  • Mein Herz Brennt (piano version)

Eisbrecher

Next up is a band called 'Eisbrecher', that I learned about through the same friend. As expected, this is also NDH as far as I'm aware. I'm fairly sure 'Das Gesetz' was one of the first songs I heard of there's, but I've more recently explored more of their stuff.

Some others I like:

  • Sturmfahrt
  • Miststück
  • Zwischen Uns
  • Verrückt
  • Automat
