Caravan Palace

'Caravan Palace' is a good example of Electro Swing. It uses old samples but mixes them in a modern way. It strays a bit further from the 'swingy' origins of the genre to lean into the modern mixing more. In their most-known song 'Lone Digger' you can hear that it's going more for the catchy-factor.

Some other songs of theirs I like:

  • Rock It For Me
  • Russian
  • Wonderland
  • Dramophone

Parov Stelar

With Parov Stelar's songs, it's more about simplistic mixing to focus more on the samples. I'd say it's closer to the origins of the genre. Their song 'Booty Swing' will definitely give a better understanding of what I mean with the samples being more of the focus.

Some other songs of theirs I like:

  • All Night
  • Jimmy's Gang
  • Clap Your Hands
  • Chambermaid Swing
