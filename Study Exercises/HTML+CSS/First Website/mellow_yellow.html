Crumb

My first recommendation in the 'Mellow Yellow' category is a band called 'Crumb'. The vibe of their best-performing song "Locket" is one I can't quite put my finger on, but it makes me emotional regardless. Combined with the music video, it's one of my favorite songs out there.

If you liked it, you might enjoy some other songs by them such as:

  • Bones
  • Part III
  • Ghostride

Jungle

'Jungle' is my second recommendation. It's more towards the groovy side of things and they always have cool and original choreography in their music videos. Their most-known song 'Casio' is how I first found out about them a few years ago and I've been following them since.

Their new album is pretty good too in my opinion, but here are some general suggestions:

  • Cherry
  • Time
  • Busy Earnin'

Half Alive

Similarly to Jungle, 'Half Alive' always has their own unique choreography, but it leans more towards interpretive dance, as you might be able to tell from their most well-known song 'still feel'.

Some other songs of theirs I like:

  • Arrow
  • Runaway
  • What's Wrong
