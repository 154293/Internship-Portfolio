About Sven

My name is Sven Keijzer and I'm a 22 year old student at Talland College in Alkmaar. In my free time I enjoy listening to music, (unsurprisingly) playing games, watching movies, hanging out with friends and some iceskating, inline skating or cycling when the weather allows it.

Music In My Life

I listen to music pretty much constantly. Not only is it something that's always been something I can rely on no matter what mood I'm in, it is something that always felt like one of the first things I developed a personal taste in. When I was younger, my mother handed me an old mp3 of hers, which I used to listen to the radio with at night before going to sleep. I ended up remembering songnammes and later looking them up on the PC downstairs in the livingroom. In short, that's kinda how my musical journey started.

My Musical Journey

As I grew up, I started listening to different types of music and it kinda happened in phases. Every so often, I'd stumble across a new style of music or genre that I'd be interested in and I'd explore adjacent music a bit, staying in that bubble for a while until I found the next thing. Of course as my range of music grew, I kept going back to stuff I used to listen to earlier as well.

Often times, music is tied to the memories that I experienced in that era of my life, which I think is a very powerful tool. They say that memories in general can be more easily remembered with the aid of mentally linking them with a specific sense. Often times, the classic example would be about how certain smells can remind you of your youth, but I actually think music is a much more common one, because who doesn't listen to music, right?
